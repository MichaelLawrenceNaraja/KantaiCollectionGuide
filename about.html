Developer and Co-owner

Larry Naraja

The developer of this website is Mr. Michael Lawrence Naraja, also known as Larry or All-rounder Teitoku, is a fresh graduate of De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde and has a degree in BS - Information Systems. He decided to make this website in order for beginners to familiarize themselves on what ships they will be encountering or rather collecting throughout the game. He started to play this game in 2015 and since then he didn't miss any major or minor events.
